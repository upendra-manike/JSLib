# LinkedIn Newsletter Cover Images Guide

## General Specifications

- **Size**: 1200 x 628 pixels (LinkedIn article cover image)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1.91:1
- **Safe Zone**: Keep important content within 1120 x 588 pixels (margins for different devices)

## Design Principles

- **Bold Typography**: Large, readable text
- **High Contrast**: Text should be clearly readable
- **Brand Colors**: Use consistent color scheme
- **Minimal Design**: Less is more - focus on clarity
- **Icon/Illustration**: Simple, relevant visual element

## Color Palette

- Primary: #0077b5 (LinkedIn Blue)
- Secondary: #0a66c2 (LinkedIn Dark Blue)
- Accent: #28a745 (Success Green) or #ff6b6b (Coral)
- Background: #ffffff or gradient
- Text: #000000 or #333333

## Font Recommendations

- Headline: Bold, Sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, Montserrat)
- Subtitle: Medium weight
- Package Name: Monospace (Fira Code, JetBrains Mono)
