## Important Notes:

1. **Newsletter Limit**: LinkedIn allows maximum 5 newsletters per member
2. **Image Requirements**: 
   - Newsletter logo/thumbnail: 300 x 300 pixels (square)
   - Article cover image: 1200 x 628 pixels (article header)
3. **Subscription**: Your connections and followers will be invited automatically
4. **Self-subscription**: You will be subscribed to your own newsletters

## Quick Setup Checklist:

- [ ] Newsletter titles ready
- [ ] Publishing frequency decided (recommend Weekly)
- [ ] Descriptions written (can be refined later)
- [ ] Cover images generated from HTML templates
- [ ] Thumbnail images created (300x300px)
- [ ] Content for first article ready

## Tips:

1. **Title**: Make it specific and searchable
2. **Description**: Highlight value proposition and target audience
3. **Frequency**: Weekly is recommended for consistent engagement
4. **Cover Images**: Use the HTML templates as starting point
5. **First Article**: Have your first newsletter article ready before creating
